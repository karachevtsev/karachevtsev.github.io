About myself

Hello! My name is Artem Karachevtsev. I am a freelance Front-End Developer from Kiev, Ukraine. Recently I am looking forward to obtain a position of Junior Front-End Developer / HTML, CSS coder with a further career advancement in a modern and trustworthy company.

I am University graduee with PhD in Optics, Laser Physics. During last two years I’ve been working hard in obtaining special skills and knowledges in front-end development sphere. I have successfully completed following courses:

  • GoIT Front-End Development Course (July 2015 – 19 December 2015);
  • GoIT Advanced Front-End Development Course (19 January 2016 – 22 March 2016);
  • C# Course with Terrasoft (6 July 2016 – 30 October 2016).

Also I have 2 years of freelance commercial experience working with clients from Ukraine, providing front-end development of websites. So today I have a good knowledge in valid, semantic, cross browser markup; development of the client side using JavaScript and jQuery (working with api jQuery plugins and writing simple scripts in JavaScript).

I use HTML5, CSS3, BEM-notation, SMACSS, Twitter Bootstrap, Sass, PostCSS, SVG, Git (command port, git bash), Gulp, JavaScript, jQuery, CMS WordPress while working over my projects. I am a well organized and punctual team player. Always highly motivated and ready to learn up-to-day web technologies, I will play a significant role in your company working process. So I am looking forward to your positive decision.

Thank you for all your considerations!
